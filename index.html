Let start the trip !

Today is a day when we travel with my girlfriend to Barcelona! Ye hey ! :-) We booked tickets and we are flying just now with Ryanair. It will take around two and half hour to get to the capital of Spain. During that time I'm tweaking my css styles and write this story. We took "Aerobus" for 10 euro in both ways. It's took about half hour to get to the city centre. When we where travelling by bus there was a lovely scenery of mountains around, and we even saw a little bit of Placa de Espanya.

day 1

Local seller

We got at "Place Catalunya", from there we got really quickly to "La Rambla" it was a very nice square to take a walk on and buy some souvenirs. We sat there at local restaurant to ate something and taste local wine Sangria. I was even approached by a local seller who sold me a wooden necklace for my girlfriend ! He priced it at 20 euros at first, and at the end when I was showing no interest in buying - I bought it for 5 euros, have a bonus of a bracelet and I even took a picture with that guy :-D

day 1

Rambla de mar

After a great night sleep and a breakfast in hotel Acta Mimic we started strolling. We took a walk towards the sea and we quickly got to the "Rambla de Mar" which is lovely port. My girlfriend shot me a nice photo even there :)

day 2

Platja Barceloneta

We had lovely boardwalk and we got to "Platja Barceloneta" which was a gorgeous beach where we spent a few hours and caught some great pictures. Behind us you could see "W" hotel where prices are starting from only 250 euro.

day 2

Street Music

I caught on video some great duet of acoustic guitar and very melodic instrument hang. We were passing near "Barri Gotic" when we found them. If we are talking about music when i was walking through the streets I recorded also one guy but only on audio format. You can listen this recordings here:

day 3

Cathedral de la Santa Eulalia

Then we got to the "Cathedral de la Santa Eulalia". And what I need to tell you, this place was truly magical and beautiful. Some guy was staying there and playing on his guitar really relaxing and very melodic music. It was lovely even just to sit there and enjoy the moment. If you planning to visit Barcelona is a -must- .
