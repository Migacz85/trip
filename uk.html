Another trip

Today is a day when we travel with my girlfriend to Barcelona! Ye hey ! :-) We booked tickets and we are flying just now with Ryanair. It will take around two and half hour to get to the Barcelona. During that time I'm tweaking my css styles and write this story. We took "Aerobus" for 10 euro in both ways. It's took about half hour to get to the city centre. When we where travelling by bus there was a lovely scenery of mountains around, and we even saw a little bit of Placa de Espanya.

Day 1

Local seller

We got at "Place Catalunya", from there we got really quickly to "La Rambla" it was a very nice square to take a walk on and buy some souvenirs. We sat there at local restaurant to ate something and taste local wine Sangria. I was even approached by a local seller who sold me a wooden necklace for my girlfriend ! He priced it at 20 euros at first, and at the end when I was showing no interest in buying - I bought it for 5 euros, have a bonus of a bracelet and I even took a picture with that guy :-D
